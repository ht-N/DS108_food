eng v2:
Job hunting after graduation remains a major challenge for many fresh graduates.
Not everyone has the opportunity to secure a position that truly aligns with their personal interests and skill sets. While several job-matching platforms exist, many still fall short in providing truly personalized recommendations.

To address this issue, our team has developed an AI-powered chatbot application that helps users discover job opportunities tailored specifically to their resumes. Users can simply upload their resumes, and the chatbot will analyze their qualifications, experience, and preferences to recommend the most relevant job openings.

The system is built using real-world job market data and leverages advanced data processing, natural language processing (NLP), and machine learning techniques to understand both user profiles and job requirements. By implementing a streamlined and intelligent matching process, our goal is to enhance the job search experience and increase the chances of job seekers finding roles that are both suitable and satisfying.

In addition, the application offers features such as skill gap analysis, personalized career advice, and resume optimization tips — all aimed at empowering graduates to make informed career decisions in a competitive job market.
